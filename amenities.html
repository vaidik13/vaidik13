Our children’s playground may be made for the kids, but even the adults can enjoy playing on the swings when they come with a beautiful view of the White Mountains.

Come check out our onsite gym! Cabot Inn offers well-equipped fitness center, allowing you to stick to your workout routine while on the road.

Coming from a long road trip? Cabot Inn offers spacious lobby with an adequate seating area to relax. Let us treat you to a cup of coffee while you watch TV or sit by window and enjoy a spectacular mountain view.

Cabot Inn offers large function room ideal for hosting weddings, family reunions, receptions, and more.

Have an upcoming business meeting or presentation? Cabot Inn offers a space for you to meet with your corporate clientele or host yearly training.
